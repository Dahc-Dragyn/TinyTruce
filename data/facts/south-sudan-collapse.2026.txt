Forensic Analysis: The South Sudan Collapse (2025-2026)
Status: Institutional Collapse / Terminal Conflict
Date: February 2026

1. Political Catalyst: The Neutralization of Riek Machar
- Arrest: First Vice President Riek Machar placed under house arrest (March 2025) following clashes in Nasir.
- Suspension: Formally suspended and charged with treason, murder, and crimes against humanity (September 2025).
- The Nasir Incident: Government alleges Machar directed the "White Army" to attack an SSPDF garrison in Nasir (March 2025), deaths > 250 soldiers.
- The Trial: Special Court for National Crimes trial in Juba (January 2026). Trial session 35.

2. Military Dynamics: Operation Enduring Peace
- Factions: SSPDF (Government) vs SPLA-IO (Machar Loyalists) vs White Army (Nuer Youth Militia).
- Conflict Zones: Yuai, Waat, Pajut (Opposition seized Dec 2025).
- SSPDF Offensive: Launched Jan 25, 2026. Evacuation of Akobo, Nyirol, and Uror.
- Key Victory: Capture of Pieri Payam (Feb 3, 2026). Capture of "Borchaar" main battle tank.
- The White Army: Decentralized Nuer youth militia. Ash-covered. Loyal to Machar but autonomous under prophets like Makuach Tut Khor.

- Revenue: 98% of budget from oil. GDP shrunk 24% in 2025.

- Debt: $13 billion oil-backed loan from UAE-based HBK DOP. Oil mortgaged until 2043.
- Inflation: 112.6% (Oct 2024). Parallel rate: 1 USD ≈ 4,544.13 SSP.

4. Regional Intervention: The Ugandan Pivot
- Actor: Uganda People's Defence Forces (UPDF).
- Role: Boosting Kiir government via joint aerial bombardments.
- Legal: UN arms embargo violation concerns; targeting ethnic Nuer communities.
